Map Information Panel:

The map to the right displays points that coordinate with locations in the Washington, DC metropolitan area. There are 4 types of places found at each point: Theater, Bar, Music, and Bike.

Look at the legend at the top right of the map! Toggle which features you want to see. The corresponding point to each type of place when you click it in the legend. Want to view just the locations of theaters? Click on "Bar," "Music," and "Bicycle," leave a checkmark only next to the theater.

This map even has fun symbols to represent each type of place! You can easily tell which point is a Bar (aka a Martini Glass) and which is a theater (aka the classic sad/happy theater masks).

I hope you enjoy the map and exploring some features of Washington DC!
